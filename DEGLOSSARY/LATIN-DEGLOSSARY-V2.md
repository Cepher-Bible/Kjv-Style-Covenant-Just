CLEMENTINE VULGATE DEGLOSSARY
Covenant Anchor Engine v1.1-EXHAUSTIVE
For the 1901 Biblia Sacra Vulgatae Editionis

Purpose
This companion preserves the Latin text while restoring covenant-native semantic structure beneath key theological terms. Each entry provides:

Latin → Engine Lock → Hebrew Root → Greek Root → Semantic Cargo

Clusters
1 = Covenant-Justice (faith, righteousness, obedience)
2 = Spirit-Breath (presence, holiness, life)
3 = Covenant-Judgment (deeds, witness, verdict)
4 = Kinsman-Purchase (atonement, redemption, inheritance)

Grammar Note (Global)
Genitive often signals covenant possession.
Dative frequently marks beneficiary.
Ablative often signals source (ex fide = from loyal-trust).
Perfect tense frequently signals completed covenant act with enduring force.
