### Purpose (Summary – not tabulated)
This companion preserves the Latin text while restoring covenant-native semantic structure beneath key theological terms.  
**Format per entry**: Latin → Engine Lock → Hebrew Root → Greek Root → Semantic Cargo  
**Clusters**:  
1 = Covenant-Justice

**Global Grammar Notes**  
- Genitive often signals covenant possession.  
- Dative frequently marks beneficiary.  
- Ablative often signals source (e.g., ex fide = from loyal-trust).  
- Perfect tense frequently signals completed covenant act with enduring force.
