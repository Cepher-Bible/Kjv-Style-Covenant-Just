# Deglossary.md

This is the canonical **Deglossary** for the **Cepher Hakam Bible - KJV-CAE (Covenant Anchor Edition)** project and the underlying **Covenant Anchor Engine v1.0.0-STABLE**.

All terms are processed through the engine's strict rules:  
- **OT direction**: Hebrew → Greek (LXX) → Latin → English (no backflow)  
- **NT direction**: Greek → Hebrew covenant-map → Latin → English  
- Immutable locks enforced (no splitting chesed/chen, polysemy preserved as sacred voltage)  
- Anchored in covenant spine before nations  
- Wisdom stack enforced (fear YHWH as beginning **and** end)  
- Output in English after tri-lock validation  

This glossary is exhaustive for the locked cords (immutable_locks) and cadence-flexible terms from the engine JSON.  
Use it for study, preaching, app integration, or repo documentation.
