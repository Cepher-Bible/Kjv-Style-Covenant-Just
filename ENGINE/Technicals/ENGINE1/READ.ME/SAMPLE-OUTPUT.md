### **Councils Era (300-800 AD)**

**Saints:**
- Martyr veneration grows (Cyprian ~250 AD)
- Prayers to saints become common (300s+)

**Verdict:** Major shifts from early church (saint prayers, Sunday, icons all normalized)

**Catholic (Rome):**
- Saint veneration + relics
- Sunday Sabbath
- Icons + statues (3D allowed)
- Papal authority supreme

**Eastern Orthodox:**
- Saint veneration + relics
- Sunday Sabbath
- Icons only (2D flat - no statues/3D)

**Shared:** Both accept Second Nicaea (787 AD - icons), both pray to saints, both Sunday

**Luther:**
- Saints: Honor but don't pray to (mediator = Christ alone)

- Saints: Honor memory only (no prayers)

- Most radical: No images, simple worship, no liturgy

- No images, no formal liturgy, believers' baptism
