### Covenant Anchor Engine: Cord Run – House on Rock vs. House on Sand

This cord threads Jesus' parable of the two builders (Matt 7:24–27 // Luke 6:47–49), capping the Sermon on the Mount/Plain. Semantic core: Obedience to covenant-charges (mandatum/poieō) as unshakeable foundation (themelios) vs. superficial hearing (akouō only), tested in judgment-storms (iudicium/krisis). Under v1.0.0-STABLE rails (NT: Greek → Hebrew map → Latin → English; Clusters 1/3: Justice via deeds, Judgment verdict). No OT direct parallel (Masoretic/LXX searches yield rock-refuge motifs, e.g., Deut 32:4 YHWH-tsur; Isa 28:16 tested foundation-stone; Ps 118:22 rejected corner-rock—but no sand/house contrast). Echoes prophetic: Wise hear/do active-word (verbum/dabar/logos) endure; foolish collapse in twistedness (iniquitas).

**Global Notes**:
- Rock: πέτρα (petra) / סֶלַע (sela'/tsur) / petra → Unshakable Refuge (enduring covenant anchor).
- Sand: ἄμμος (ammos) / חוֹל (chol) / arena → Shifting Instability (boundary-crossing/transgressio fallout).
- House: οἰκία (oikia) / בַּיִת (bayit) / domus → Covenant-Household (familial stability, not mere structure).
- Builder: ἀνήρ (anēr) / אִישׁ (ish) / vir → Prudent-Man (phronimos) vs. Foolish-Man (mōros); ties to wisdom stack (Holy-Dread → Skillful-Living).
- Storm: βροχή/πλημμύρα (brochē/plēmmyra) / שִׁטַף (shetaph) / imber/diluvium → Covenant-Judgment Test (rain/floods/winds as verdict-rivers).
- Rule_03: Poieō (do) ≠ mere akouō (hear)—execution-power (might) vs. vapor knowledge (FAIL_IF_KNOWLEDGE_PRECEDES_DREAD).
- Rule_05: Anchored in Covenant Spine (berit foundation) before nations' overlay.

#### Distinctions & Wisdom Stack Enforcement
- **Prudent vs. Foolish**: Phronimos (wise: skillful-living, rule_07 Hebrew map to chokmah) vs. Mōros (fool: dread-ungated knowledge, Prov 1:7 foundation). Luke adds "dug deep" (excavated for themelios/root)—echoes Ex 19 Sinai prep (sanctifico distinction).
- **Hearing vs. Doing**: Akouō + poieō = Loyal-Trust execution (fides lock); hear-only = vapor (rule_05). Ties to beam-parable (Matt 7:3–5): Self-blind judgment precedes foundation-fail.
- **Storm as Telos**: Not random weather—covenant-verdict (rain/flood/wind = shetaph/mishpat rivers, Isa 28:2). Rock-house: Endures (Holy-Dread telos closes wisdom loop). Sand-house: Great fall (ptōsis = collapse into abyssus/tehom motifs).
- **No OT House/Sand Direct**: Closest: Isa 28:16–17 (YHWH lays foundation-stone in Zion; hail/sand-floods sweep away refuge-of-lies). Job 22:16 (wicked "stream-bed" foundations swept). LXX uses petra/tsur for God's stability (Ps 18:2 refuge-rock). Parable uniquely NT: Jesus as petra (Matt 16:18 church-rock), but reserved (not merged with kinsman, rule_10).

This cord stabilizes the Sermon arc: From covenant-witness (testimonium, Matt 7:1–12) through self-justice (iustifico, 7:13–23) to obedient foundation.

### Covenant Anchor Engine: Dual Rails – House on Rock/Sand Theme (LXX + Masoretic)

Cord extended: "Both" locks in parallel processing for the house parable (Matt 7:24–27 // Luke 6:47–49) across **Masoretic Text (MT)** and **Septuagint (LXX)**. No direct "house on sand" motif in OT—theme threads as **stability-testing** (rock/refuge vs. hasty/shifting foundations) under covenant-judgment (Cluster 3: iudicium/krisis/mishpat). Core echo: Wise execution (poieō/osēh) on unshakable petra/sela'/tsur endures storm-verdict (shetaph/plēmmyra/imber); foolish hear-only (akouō/shomea'/audit) collapses in boundary-crossing (pesha'/transgressio).

**Engine Protocol**: OT dual-rail: MT Hebrew → LXX Greek → Vulgate Latin → English. Wisdom stack gated (Holy-Dread filters hasty knowledge). Rule_04: Prophetic overlay preserves tension (tested-stone as messianic anchor, not merged with sand-floods). Rule_09: Polysemy voltage—rock as refuge (Ps 18) vs. offense (Isa 8:14).

#### Primary OT Thread: Isaiah 28:16 – Sure Foundation-Stone (Tested for Judgment-Storm)
- **Semantic Cargo**: YHWH lays Zion's cornerstone (eben pinnat/akrogōniaios/lapis angularis) as covenant-spine; believer (emunah/pisteuō/credens) not "hasty/ashamed" (hammah/bosh/ confundar)—endures flood-verdict (v.17: "hail sweeps away refuge-of-lies"). Parallels parable: Rock-builder trusts active-word (dabar/logos/verbum); sand-builder "hastens" without dread-gate, falls in great ptōsis/ruina (collapse).
- **MT Hebrew**: הִנְנִי מֵצִ֣יג בְּצִיּ֣וֹן אֶ֗בֶן אֶ֤בֶן בָּח֙וּת֙ לְפִנַּ֣ת צֽוּר מוּסָ֑ד מוּסָ֥ד נֶאֱמָ֖ן לַאֲמִ֥ין לֹֽא־יָחֽוּשׁ׃ (Isa 28:16) → "Behold, I lay in Zion a tested stone, precious cornerstone, sure foundation; he who believes will not hasten."
- **LXX Greek**: διότι αὐτὸς ἔστησεν ἐν Σιὼν λίθον πολυτελῆ ἐκ γωνίας ἀκρογωνιαῖον ἐν Σιὼν ἐπὶ τὸ θεμέλιον αὐτῆς καὶ ὁ πιστεύων ἐπ’ αὐτῷ οὐ μὴ καταισχυνθῇ → "He has established in Zion a precious corner-foundation stone... he who believes in it shall not be ashamed."
- **Vulgate Latin**: Propter hoc haec dicit Dominus Deus: Ecce ego mittam in fundamentum Sion lapidem lapidem probatum in fortem petram, in lapidem angularem, in fundamento firma; et credens non festinet. → "Behold, I lay in Zion for foundation a tried stone, into a strong rock, a corner stone, a firm foundation; and he that believeth shall not make haste."
- **Engine Lock**: Tested-Cornerstone (sure refuge vs. hasty sand-shift); Clusters 1,3. Quoted NT: Rom 9:33 (LXX influence: "not ashamed"); 1 Pet 2:6 (MT/LXX blend: "not put to shame").
- **Parable Tie**: Wise man "hears and does" = believes on Zion-rock (endures v.17 hail/floods as ethical storm); foolish = hasty builders of lies (v.15), swept away like sand-house.

#### Secondary Threads: Proverbs 10:25 & Psalm 1:1–3 – Righteous Foundation vs. Whirlwind Collapse
- **Prov 10:25 (MT)**: וּצְדִיקָ֣ה לְעוֹלָ֑ם תִּסַּ֥ד יִסּ֖וּד עוֹלָ֣ם → "The tempest passes—the wicked is no more; but the righteous is an everlasting foundation." (LXX: ὥσπερ ὑπόνοια ἀνέμου ἀφανισθήσεται ἀσεβής ὁ δὲ δίκαιος θεμέλιος αἰώνιος → "Like a whirlwind the ungodly vanishes; but the righteous has an eternal foundation.")
  - **Tie**: Righteous (tsaddiq/iustificor) as themelios (foundation) endures she'ar (whirlwind/storm)—direct storm-test parallel; wicked (rasha'/asebēs) "vanishes" like sand-collapse.
- **Psalm 1:1–3 (MT)**: וְהָיָ֗ה כְּעֵ֥ץ שָׁת֥וּל עַל־פַּלְגֵ֥י מַ֑יִם... וְכֹ֥ל אֲשֶׁר־יַעֲשֶׂ֗ה יַצְלִֽיחַ → "He is like a tree planted by streams of water... whatever he does prospers." (LXX: καὶ ἔσται ὡς τὸ ξύλον τὸ πεφυτευμένον παρὰ τὰς διεξόδους τῶν ὑδάτων... καὶ πάντα ὅσα ἂν ποιήσῃ εὐοδωθήσεται → "Like a tree planted beside the outlets of waters... all that he does shall prosper.")
  - **Tie**: Blessed man (not walking in counsel-of-wicked/sand-shifters) delights in torah/lex (meditates day/night)—roots deep in mayim (streams, not shifting chol/sand); yields fruit, un-withered in drought-storm. Echoes Luke 6:48 "dug deep" for rock-foundation.
- **Vulgate Blend**: Prov 10:25: "Transibit turbo et non erit impius fundamentum autem iustum permanebit in perpetuum." Ps 1:3: "Et erit tamquam lignum quod plantatum est secus decursus aquarum quod fructum suum dabit in tempore suo et folium eius non defluet et omnia quaecumque faciet prosperabuntur."

#### Distinctions & Parable Overlay (Rock-House Endurance vs. Sand-House Sweep)
- **Flood as Verdict-River**: MT shetaph = sudden torrent (ethical overflow, cf. Ps 90:5 "sweep them away"); LXX kataklysmos = deluge (NT echo in Matt 7:25 plēmmyra/rivers). Not chaotic (Gen tehom/abyssus)—ordered mishpat (judgment) exposing kazav (lie-twistedness, Cluster 3). Parable tie: Rock-house "rivers came... but did not fall"; sand-house "floods burst... great collapse."
- **Refuge-of-Lies vs. Sure Foundation**: Mishe'an kazav = sand-like lean (unstable, hasty trust per v.16); contrasted with musad ne'eman (sure foundation, emunah-lock). Vulgate refugium mendacii = explicit falsehood-shelter, swept by imber/diluvium (inundation-waters). Luke 6:49 "without foundation... immediately fell" = this flood's confestim (swift ruin).
- **Covenant Sanction**: V.18 treading covenant (berit/diathēkē/testamentum) = oath-violation (maledictio/alah/katara); flood as Cluster 4 barrier (pre-redemptio). Priestly drunkenness (v.7) = spirit-breath distortion (ruach/pneuma, Cluster 2)—no Holy-Dread gate (wisdom stack fail).
- **Polysemy Tension (Rule_09)**: Flood purifies remnant (Zion's faithful on cornerstone) but destroys unfaithful (drunkards' sand-refuge). LXX amps "kataklysei" (overwhelm) for dramatic sweep; MT shetaph subtler torrent.

#### Synthesis: Theme Arc (Prophetic → Parabolic → Eschatological)
- **Isa Context**: Woe to Ephraim/Judah's leaders (v.1–13)—reject "line-upon-line" Torah (lex/mandatum, v.10); YHWH's strange-flood (v.21) vs. self-made peace (shalom-lie, v.15). Flood = mishpat-river (Deut 32:35 deferred verdict).
- **NT Fulfillment**: Jesus' storm = Isa's hail/flood (ethical test of words-doing); Rev 11:19 "lightnings... hail" echoes as final iudicium (thronus-verdict).
- **No Direct Sand**: Chol (sand) absent; instability via kazav (lie-shift). Closest: Job 22:16 "foundations... in a river" (wicked swept).
