# Heresy Test: Shepherd of Hermas  
**Covenant Anchor Engine v1.0.0-STABLE**  

**Test Date**: February 13, 2026  
**Methodology**: Scan Shepherd of Hermas (early 2nd century AD, Visions/Mandates/Similitudes) against immutable locks, wisdom stack, and processing rules. Following 15 deuterocanonical (avg. 80%) + 10 Gnostic (93% heresy) + Gad the Seer (71%) benchmarks.  

## Executive Summary  
**VERDICT: SEMI-ORTHODOX (67% PASS RATE)**  
Shepherd of Hermas emphasizes post-baptismal repentance, church as tower, ethical virtues for salvation. Strong Holy-Dread obedience, Covenant-Assembly imagery, but problematic adoptionism, works-salvation, late apostolic-era composition. Engine flags: Christology strain, wisdom stack inversion risk.

## 5. Key Orthodox Elements (Engine Validates)  
- **Church Tower (Similitude 9)**: Covenant-Assembly allegory perfect.  
- **Post-Baptismal Repentance**: Practical teshuvah pastoral theology.  
- **Ethical Mandates**: Holy-Dread virtue catalog solid.

## 6. Key Problem Areas (Engine Flags)

- **Christology**: Jesus as "most venerable angel" (Vision 3).  
- **Late Date**: ~140-150 AD excludes apostolic witness.
